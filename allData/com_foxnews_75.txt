‘American Chopper’ Returns with New Season on Discovery Channel
 
Play Audio Clip Listen to audio clip.

The cast of 'American Chopper' stopped by to spend "A Few Moments With" FOX News' Jacquelyn Karl to discuss the return of their reality series on Discovery Channel. The show chronicles the lives of Paul Teutul Sr. and his sons Paul Teutul Jr. and Mikey as they build incredible motorcycles and operate their business'. The Teutuls shared with Jacquelyn what they have learned in the over 15 years since their series premiered and how they work together in business and in family. The new season of 'American Chopper' premiers Monday, May 28th at 10 P.M.
