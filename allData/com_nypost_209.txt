Inside the Great British Tattoo Show
 
A man has a tattoo of Jared Leto's Joker from Suicide Squad applied to his torso during the Great British Tattoo Show at Alexandra Palace in London, England. The show hosts over 300 tattoo artists where members of the public can walk in and get a tattoo or watch others in the open exhibition space.
