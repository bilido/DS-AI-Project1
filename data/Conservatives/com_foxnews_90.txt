Senator Bill Cassidy’s Response To Rep. Adam Schiff’s “Throw The Bums Out Comment”: Americans Want Prosperity, Not Doom & Gloom Socialism
 
Play Audio Clip Listen to audio clip.

"Adam Schiff is right, we should go back to where our economy is growing at 1% a year in which people are leaving the work force in which wages are not growing, the stock market is flat. That's what we want to go back to? Don't think so Adam, we want prosperity, not doom and gloom socialism."

---Senator Cassidy responding to Rep Adam Schiff saying "Throw the bums out"

Senator Dr. Bill Cassidy (R-LA) joined Brian Kilmeade today and responded to Rep Adam Schiff saying we need to "throw the bums out" in order to protect the Mueller probe by saying, "We want prosperity, not doom and gloom socialism," and went on to point out the accomplishments under President Trump. Cassidy also discussed his new policy ideas to lower health care costs and the renewed push for DACA reform in congress.
