Get to know Singapore before it hosts historic peace summit
 
The Merlion statue sits lit up in front of the city skyline in Singapore. The space-starved city, recently voted the world's most expensive place to live for the fifth year in a row, will host the upcoming North Korea-US peace summit next month, according to President Donald Trump. The meeting between North Korean leader Kim Jong Un and Trump will take place June 12.
