Refugees and migrants rescued at sea
 
Refugees and migrants are rescued by members of the Spanish NGO Proactiva Open Arms after they left Libya trying to reach European soil aboard an overcrowded rubber boat, north of the Libyan coast. In total, 105 refugees and migrants from various countries, including Bangladesh, Egypt, Nigeria, Morocco, Ghana, Pakistan, Sudan, Libya, Eritrea and Senegal, were rescued.
