Anti-Terrorism Efforts Ramp Up In London
 
Police weapons are displayed in a police car during a media opportunity in London, Wednesday, Aug. 3, 2016. London's police force is putting more armed officers on the streets 'to protect against the threat of terrorism.'' The increase in the number of officers follows attacks in France, Belgium and Germany. (AP Photo/Kirsty Wigglesworth)
