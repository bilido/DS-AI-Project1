Suicide Bomber in Somalia
 
United Nations office guards carry the dead body of their colleague, who was killed in a suicide car bomb, outside the UN's office in Mogadishu, Somalia, Tuesday, July 26, 2016. A suicide bomber detonated an explosives-laden car outside the United Nations Mine Action Service offices in Mogadishu, killing 13 people, including seven U.N. guards, a Somali police official said.
